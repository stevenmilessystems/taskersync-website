AI assistants have gone from novelty to essential tool for busy professionals. But there's a big difference between AI that answers questions and AI that actually does your tasks.

The Problem with Most AI Assistants

ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are powerful - but they require you to go to them. You open the app, type your question, read the response, then figure out what to do with it. That's still friction. The best AI works in the background and delivers results where you already look.
