How to Get More Done in Less Time - Proven Strategies for 2026

By TaskerSync · March 2026 · 6 min read

Most productivity advice assumes you need to work harder or longer. The better question is: what if you could eliminate entire categories of work?

Strategy 1: Automate Research and Drafting

Research and drafting are the two biggest time sinks for knowledge workers. TaskerSync connects to Google Tasks and automatically researches, drafts, and answers your tasks - writing results back into your notes in seconds. This single change eliminates hours per week.

Strategy 2: Time Blocking

Schedule specific times for specific types of work. Email from 9-9:30am. Deep work from 10am-12pm. Meetings in the afternoon. Working on the right type of task at the right time doubles output.

Strategy 3: Task Batching

Group similar tasks and do them together. All calls in one session. All emails in one session. All writing in one session. Context switching is expensive - batching eliminates it.

Strategy 4: Ruthless Prioritisation

At the start of every day, identify your one most important task. Do it first, before email, before meetings, before anything else. Everything else is a bonus.

Strategy 5: Eliminate, Not Just Optimise

The highest-leverage question isn't "how do I do this faster?" It's "do I need to do this at all?" Every week, look at your task list and ask what can be deleted, delegated, or deferred indefinitely.

Strategy 6: Protect Recovery Time

Deep work requires recovery. An hour of focused work after a genuine break outperforms three hours of tired slogging every time.
