Best Google Tasks Alternatives in 2026

By TaskerSync · March 2026 · 6 min read

If you're looking for Google Tasks alternatives, you're probably frustrated with something specific - missing features, limited views, or wanting more power. Here's an honest comparison.

Todoist

Best for: Power users who want filters, priorities, labels, and project management. Natural language input, project views, team collaboration. Free tier is generous; paid ~$5/month unlocks reminders and integrations.

TickTick

Best for: People who want a calendar view alongside their tasks. Combines task management with a built-in calendar and habit tracker. The Pomodoro timer is a nice bonus.

Things 3 (Apple only)

Best for: Apple ecosystem users who want a beautiful, opinionated system. One-time purchase (~$50 Mac, ~$10 iOS). No subscription. Deep Siri and Shortcuts integration.

Microsoft To Do

Best for: Microsoft 365 users. Free, deep integration with Outlook and Teams. The "My Day" feature is genuinely useful for daily planning.
